The Problem

AI can answer general finance questions, but not yours. Your financial life is scattered across banks, mutual funds, and more. No AI can give you truly personalized advice—until now.

Our Solution

FinSim is a conversational financial simulator that connects to Fi Money’s MCP Server, securely aggregates your real financial data, and uses Google Gemini to deliver actionable, explainable insights—right when you need them.

What Makes Us Unique?

  • Conversational scenario simulation: “What if I retire at 50?”—see instant projections.
  • Explainable AI: Every suggestion comes with a plain-language rationale.
  • Privacy-first: Real-time dashboard to control, export, or revoke your data.
  • Unified experience: Chat, simulate, and visualize—all in one place.

Features

  • Natural language Q&A and scenario simulation
  • Personalized financial insights and projections
  • Visual dashboards and instant charts
  • Goal-based planning and progress tracking
  • Anomaly and opportunity detection
  • Multi-source financial data aggregation

How It Works

  1. Connect your accounts securely via Fi MCP
  2. Ask questions or simulate scenarios in natural language
  3. Our agent fetches and analyzes your data using Google Gemini
  4. Get actionable insights, visualizations, and full control over your data
