The Problem

Why current AI financial tools fall short

Most AI financial tools today operate in isolation, without access to real user data. They provide generic advice that doesn't reflect your actual financial situation, spending patterns, or personal goals.

Without access to your real financial data, these tools can only offer:

  • Generic financial advice that doesn't apply to your situation
  • One-size-fits-all recommendations
  • Impersonal responses that lack context
  • Limited ability to track your progress over time

Our Solution

Personalized AI that understands your real financial data

Our AI agent connects directly to your financial data through Fi's MCP server, providing truly personalized insights and recommendations based on your actual spending, saving, and investment patterns.

With access to your real financial data, you can ask questions like:

  • "How much did I spend on dining out last month compared to groceries?"
  • "What's my current savings rate and how does it compare to my goal?"
  • "Which categories are my biggest spending areas this quarter?"
  • "How much should I save monthly to reach my vacation goal by December?"
  • "What's my investment portfolio performance and what should I adjust?"
  • "Am I on track to meet my retirement savings target?"

Get instant, personalized answers based on your actual financial data, not generic advice.
